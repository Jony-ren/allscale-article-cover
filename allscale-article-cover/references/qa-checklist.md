# QA Checklist

## Pass Criteria

- Wide horizontal cover.
- White dotted editorial canvas uses `assets/brand/dot-bg.jpg` directly.
- Thin black outer border with 40px rounded corners and 60% corner smoothing.
- AllScale logo in the upper-left uses `assets/brand/allscale-logo.png` directly.
- AllScale logo sits close to the main headline, forming a clear upper-left cluster.
- Small green brand accents, not a green-dominant final background.
- Huge readable black Playfair Display SemiBold headline in exactly 2 lines.
- Headline line-height equals headline font size.
- AllScale logo visible width is approximately 2.5 headline letters.
- Handwritten note, if used, is set in Figma Hand.
- 2-3 curated collage assets, not a moodboard.
- One rounded photo card, usually lower-right.
- Lower-right photo card has no green edge, green corner, green matte, source-board background, separator, or margin.
- Lower-right photo card right margin equals its bottom margin.
- Grayscale object cutouts feel premium and coherent.
- Upper-right and lower-left objects are two different choices from `assets/examples/object-pack.png`.
- Selected object-pack icons are not redesigned, redrawn, stylized, recolored, or replaced.
- Layout has whitespace and strong hierarchy.
- Every major element has clear breaking space from neighboring elements and from the outer border.

## Fail Signals

- Headline is tiny, centered weakly, or secondary to assets.
- Headline is not Playfair Display SemiBold.
- Headline line-height is larger or smaller than the headline font size.
- Logo is too large or too small compared with the headline; it should be approximately 2.5 headline letters wide.
- Handwritten note is not Figma Hand.
- Headline is 1 line, 3 lines, or otherwise not exactly 2 lines.
- Text is misspelled, garbled, warped, or unreadable.
- Looks like a generic crypto ad, SaaS hero, infographic, pitch deck, or social meme.
- Too many images/cards.
- Dotted grid is missing, redrawn, recolored, restyled, regenerated, or not sourced from `assets/brand/dot-bg.jpg`.
- No border, or the border has sharp corners, or the 40px rounded corners lack 60% corner smoothing and look too stiff.
- Logo area is missing, huge, distorted, noisy, redrawn, recolored, restyled, regenerated, or not sourced from `assets/brand/allscale-logo.png`.
- Logo is isolated too far from the main headline, instead of sitting close to it like the reference.
- Green background dominates the final cover.
- Photo is full-bleed instead of a rounded card.
- Lower-right photo card shows green edge, green corner, green matte, source-board background, separator, or margin.
- Lower-right photo card right margin and bottom margin do not match.
- Elements touch, overlap, crowd, or lack the reference's breaking space.
- Objects are colorful when they should be grayscale.
- Upper-right or lower-left objects are not selected from `object-pack.png` when suitable options exist.
- Selected object-pack icons are redesigned, redrawn, stylized, recolored, or replaced.

## Fixes

- If it is too busy: remove assets until only one upper-right object, one lower-left object, and one photo card remain.
- If it is not branded: add the AllScale wordmark and green bracket accents.
- If it looks generic: emphasize dotted white canvas, huge serif headline, grayscale collage objects, and thin black outer border with 40px radius and 60% corner smoothing.
- If text is wrong: regenerate with fewer words and stricter spelling instructions.
- If the headline is not two lines: rewrite to a shorter two-line headline and regenerate.
- If green dominates: return to white background and keep green only as accents.
