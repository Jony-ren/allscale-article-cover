# Style DNA

## Must Have

- Wide horizontal editorial cover, ideally 16:9.
- White or near-white canvas with a subtle gray dotted grid.
- Thin black outer border near the edge with 40px rounded corners and 60% corner smoothing. The corner shape should feel soft and continuous, not stiff or mechanically circular.
- AllScale logo in the upper-left, placed close to the main headline.
- Small green corner-bracket accents near the logo; use AllScale green `#16D36B` or a close vivid green.
- Huge black Playfair Display SemiBold headline, left-dominant, exactly 2 lines.
- Headline line-height equals headline font size.
- Strong typographic hierarchy: headline first, objects second.
- Editorial collage assets: grayscale classical/finance objects plus one rounded photo card.
- Large whitespace; avoid filling every corner.
- Clear breaking space between every major element, matching the target-cover reference.

## Typography

- Use Playfair Display SemiBold for the headline.
- Headline should be black, oversized, exactly 2 lines, and occupy roughly 40-60% of the cover width.
- Headline line-height must be exactly 1.0: line-height equals font size. Avoid loose leading.
- Scale the AllScale logo so its visible width is approximately 2.5 headline letters at the chosen headline font size.
- Use title case or sentence case based on the article title.
- Avoid tiny long subtitles. If a note is needed, use a short handwritten phrase near the bottom-left set in Figma Hand.

## Layout Pattern

- Upper-left: AllScale logo close to the headline, not isolated far above it.
- Left/middle-left: huge headline in exactly two lines.
- Upper-right: one object selected from `assets/examples/object-pack.png`, chosen to fit the article.
- Lower-left: a second, different object selected from `assets/examples/object-pack.png`, chosen to fit the article.
- Lower-right: rounded rectangle photo card with subtle green-tinted editorial imagery, but no green edge or green source-board background around the rounded photo.
- Lower-right photo card: right margin to the outer border must equal bottom margin to the outer border.
- Bottom-left or under a lower-left object: optional handwritten short phrase with underline.

## Spacing

- Use the reference cover's airy breaking space as a layout requirement, not a suggestion.
- Keep a visible gap between logo and headline while keeping them visually close as one upper-left cluster.
- Keep clear separation between headline and upper-right object.
- Keep clear separation between headline and lower-left object.
- Keep clear separation between lower-left object and handwritten note.
- Keep clear separation between the lower-right photo card and surrounding title/object areas.
- Match the lower-right photo card's right margin and bottom margin exactly or as close as visually possible.
- Keep all major elements comfortably inside the outer border.
- If any element touches, overlaps, crowds, or visually merges with another element, the layout fails.

## Color

- Dominant: white background and black typography.
- Accent: AllScale green `#16D36B` for corner marks or small brand details.
- Dots: soft gray.
- Objects: mostly grayscale.
- Photo card: can use muted natural greens.
- Do not make the entire background green unless the user asks for a source-material board rather than the final cover.
- Do not let the lower-right photo card expose green source-board edges or green corner mats.

## Prohibitions

- No generic SaaS gradient hero.
- No full-bleed stock photo cover.
- No busy infographic, chart, dashboard, or UI screenshot.
- No cartoon, mascot, 3D toy, or cute illustration.
- No neon crypto poster.
- No dark-mode cyberpunk look.
- No centered logo-only brand slide.
- No cluttered grid of many asset cards in the final cover.
- No illegible or garbled headline text.
